📌 Project Overview

The Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to analyze sales performance, identify trends, and generate actionable business insights. It helps in understanding key metrics such as revenue, profit, and regional performance.



\---

\-Data cleaning \& transformation

\-Data modeling

\-KPI creation using DAX

\-Interactive dashboard design

\-Decomposition Trees analysis

\-Smart Narratives

\-Anomaly Detection

\-Performance optimization in Power BI

🔍 Key Insights

\-Revenue Growth: Steady upward trend from 2020–2022, with monthly revenue up 3.3% over the prior month.
-Profitability: Strong margins, total profit at $10.5M with consistent growth.
-Customer Insights: 17.4K unique customers, average revenue per customer $1,431; skilled manual and professional roles drive most orders.
-Product Performance: Tires and Tubes most ordered; Shorts have highest return rate.
-Returns: Overall return rate 2.2%, with Bikes category highest at 3.08%.
-Geography: Major order volumes from US, Germany, France, UK.
-Key Influencers: Married customers, graduate degrees, and mid-income ranges show higher likelihood of homeownership.
